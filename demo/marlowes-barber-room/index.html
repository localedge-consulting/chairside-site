The barber

I left the rush on purpose.

I’m Marlowe. I spent years in a four-chair shop being told to turn the seat faster, and I couldn’t do my best work that way. So now it’s just me, by appointment, out of Rosewood Studio. You get the whole hour and my full attention — no rotation, no being passed off, no “close enough.”

Everyone’s welcome in my chair, and every head of hair gets the same care, whether it’s your first visit or your fiftieth.

How I work

What the whole hour buys

Most chairs are built for speed. Mine’s built for getting it exactly right — whatever’s on your head.

01

Every texture

Curly, coily, wavy, fine, thick — I cut to how your hair actually behaves, not one default method. If other shops have left you with a cut that “doesn’t sit right,” this is usually why.

02

A real consultation

Before anything, we figure out what you actually want — photos welcome — and what’ll work with your hair and how it grows. No guessing, no surprises in the mirror.

03

Time to do it right

No one’s waiting behind you, so I can take the cut slowly, check it from every angle, and fix the millimeter that’s bugging me. That’s the difference you feel a week later.

04

The cut, not a compromise

You leave with exactly what we agreed on — not whatever the line had time for. If it’s not right, we keep going. The hour is yours.
