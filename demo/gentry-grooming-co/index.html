The ritual

What a proper grooming looks like

A great cut is half of it. Here’s the rest — the part most chairs skip.

01

The consultation

Before a blade touches your hair, we talk: how you wear it, what your week looks like, what you’re growing out or cutting back. The cut starts with listening.

02

The cut

Scissor-over-comb and clipper work, taken at the pace it deserves. No two heads are the same, and I cut to yours — your growth, your part, your face shape.

03

The straight razor

A hot towel to soften the skin, a proper lather, and a straight-razor finish on the neck and edges. It’s the closest, cleanest line there is — and it’s a dying art.

04

The finish

Hot towel, a touch of tonic, and you leave put-together from every angle. You’ll notice it in the mirror at home three days later.
