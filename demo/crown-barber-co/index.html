Why they stay

What twenty-five years gets you

Anyone can give you one good cut. Here’s what a barber who’s done this for decades gives you instead.

01

The same cut, every time

No “who did you go to last time?” I keep your cut in my head. You sit down, I already know the guard, the part, and the way your crown sits.

02

I’ll tell you the truth

If a style won’t suit your face or your hair, I’ll say so — and show you the one that will. Twenty-five years means I’ve seen what works and what just looks good on someone else.

03

It grows out right

A good cut isn’t just day one. I cut so it still looks sharp at week three, so you’re not back in the chair before you’re ready.

04

You’re not a number

One barber, one chair. I’m not rushing you to flip the seat. We talk, you relax, and you leave looking like yourself on your best day.
